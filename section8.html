Data Privacy and Security

Our Commitment

We at Health Guard are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. We understand the importance of maintaining the confidentiality of your information and take all necessary measures to safeguard it.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information that is necessary for providing our services to you. This may include personal information such as your name, email address, age, gender, and health-related data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to personalize your experience, provide you with relevant services and support, improve our products and services, and communicate with you about updates and new features.

Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. This includes encryption, firewalls, and secure data storage practices.

Third-Party Services

At times, we may engage third-party service providers to assist us in delivering our services. These providers are contractually obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your data.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update our privacy policy as needed. Any changes will be communicated to you through our website or other appropriate channels. By continuing to use our services, you agree to the updated privacy policy.
